The Fun Life

Don't know what to do on a day off? Call your friends and try one of the activities listed below!

Besides ice skating, many people like to go roller skating during the summer and spring! A great place to go would be the Pier 2 Roller Rink. It is located in Brooklyn and has a very nice view of the Manhatten.

NYC is known for having the best thrift stores. One of the most popular thrift stores is Cure Thrift Shop located in Manhatten. They sell clothes, furniture, and other home items.

Bowling is one of the most funnest things to do with your friends on a weekend. A great place to cheak out is BOWLMOR Lanes. It is located in Times Square and many celebrities have gone there like Lady Gaga and Kim Kardashian.

How cool is it to be able to skateboard in a city like New York? LES Coleman Skatepark is definitely the hotspot for skateboarding. The large size of the park keeps it from feeling too crowded. It is located in Manhatten.

Watching a movie in a regular movie theater is great, but imagine watching it in 4D! It's wild! You get to feel like you're in the movie! Regal E-Walk 4DX & RPX located in Manhatten is a theater complex with multiple screens featuring new release films, plush seating & concession stand.

Riding ferries along the East River is definitely a magical experience, especcially at night when you can see all the skyscrapers in Manhatten lit up.
