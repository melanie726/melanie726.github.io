Joe's Pizza

Famous Joe's Pizza (or “JP,” as their court submissions call it) has been featured in numerous movies and in TV shows like “Law & Order” and “Keeping Up With the Kardashians.” In 1996, New York Magazine described its pizza as “the quintessential New York slice.”

Nobu

Nobuyuki "Nobu" Matsuhisa is a Japanese celebrity chef and restaurateur known for his fusion cuisine blending traditional Japanese dishes with Peruvian ingredients. His signature dish is black cod in miso. He has restaurants bearing his name in several countries.
